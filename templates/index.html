What PaperMind does

Everything you need to
explore research, faster

From discovery to deep analysis — a full pipeline powered by state-of-the-art AI, built for researchers and builders.

arXiv Search

Query millions of preprints across computer science, physics, math, and more. Real-time results straight from arXiv's open API.

Semantic Embeddings

Papers are encoded into dense vectors using Sentence Transformers, capturing meaning beyond simple keyword matching.

FAISS Vector Store

Embeddings are indexed with Facebook's FAISS for sub-millisecond similarity search across thousands of processed documents.

RAG Q&A

Ask any question about a paper and get grounded, accurate answers via Retrieval-Augmented Generation — no hallucinations.

Groq LLM

Ultra-fast inference powered by Groq's LPU hardware, delivering answers in under two seconds — even for complex research questions.

PDF Processing

Download, chunk, and process any arXiv paper automatically. One click turns a PDF into a fully queryable knowledge base.
